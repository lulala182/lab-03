Solo Leveling



follows Jinwoo Sung, a weak hunter in a world where people fight monsters in dungeons. After a near-death experience, he gains a mysterious system that lets him level up infinitely. As he grows stronger, he uncovers secrets about the dungeons, his powers, and the true nature of his world.
