The Apothecary Diaries

(Kusuriya no Hitorigoto) is a historical mystery light novel, manga, and anime series written by Natsu Hyuuga. It follows the story of Maomao, a sharp-minded apothecary who is kidnapped and forced to work as a low-ranking servant in the imperial palace's rear court, where the emperor's concubines reside.
